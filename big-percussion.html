The Bloco Explosão was formed for the first time in 1998 on the occasion of the Carnival of Cultures in Berlin as an amalgamation of various samba music groups (including Vila Cruz, Palpitações, Tamborada) all under the musical direction of Volker Conrath .

Today it is first of all a samba drum formation with up to 30 players, which is now well known far beyond Berlin. One of our annual highlights is still the Carnival of Cultures in Berlin, which we mentioned several times as one of the largest formations in the first few years. In 2003 and again in 2012 and 2019 we were award winners in the "Overall Formation" category. In 2018 we celebrated our 20th anniversary at the carnival.

Something else

Bloco Explosão, that is the fiery samba percussion formation with people from Berlin and the rest of the world who love to play and dance. With its colors red and yellow, Bloco Explosão stands for joie de vivre, light, sun, but also for fire and explosiveness.
