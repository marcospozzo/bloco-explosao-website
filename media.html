Explosão - plus Friends & Band

The 2nd CD is here!


Inspired by the large Afroblocos from Bahia (Brazil). Because of our ever-narrowing Contacts there, this CD builds a bridge between Berlin and Brazil.
So we are a little proud that there are 3 Mestre (musical directors) of the most famous ones there Groups play as "special guests" in various pieces on our CD:

  • Marivaldo Paim (Ilê Aiyê)
  • Memeu Nunes (Olodum)
  • Mario Bomba (Muzenza)

And as a guest musician from Rio de Janeiro: Jovi Joviniano

The Berlin music scene is also represented on our CD with exceptionally good musicians.
(Selection):

  • Renato Pantera (Rio de Janeiro / Berlin): vocals, guitar
  • Hans Limburg (Berlin): vocals, guitar
  • Gui (Rio de Janeiro / Berlin): bass
  • Georg Pfister, Oliver Fox, Thomas Walter Maria: saxophones, flute
  • Jan Gropper, Greg Ambrosine: Trumpets
  • Martin Hansel: Solo guitar

Let yourself be enchanted by fiery Brazilian rhythms and by the strength and joie de vivre.
convince the music.

Berlin percussive, Samba & more

The first Bloco Explosão CD


Both CDs can be heard on all streaming services. They can be ordered from us (10 € per CD) or downloaded from Amazon. At Soundcloud you can listen to excerpts.

The 1st CD “Berlin percussive; Samba & more ”takes you on a musical journey from Berlin Salvador da Bahia to Rio. All of the pieces are purely percussive.

Guest musicians from Brazil are Marivaldo Paim (Ilê Aiyê) from Salvador da Bahia and Jovi Joviniano from Rio de Janeiro.
